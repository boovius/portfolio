Bio

Joshua Book is a full stack developer, specializing in back-end development and software architecture.


Joshua is a recent graduate of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program in Los Angeles. He comes from the world of film and television production where he has an extensive background in creative producing and project management. He has dabbled in video game design, political activism, and community organizing.


Joshua's mission is to create innovative technology that empowers and connects people all over the world.
