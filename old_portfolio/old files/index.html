Is Who

Joshua is a producer, concept designer, and aspiring entrepreneur. Growing up in Long Island, Joshua moved to New York City after high school to attend NYU.

After college, Joshua honed his aesthetic sensibilities and project management techniques through an extensive background in filmmaking and television production, where he brought to life narratives that had powerful impacts on their audiences.

Joshua spent some formative time working and volunteering in the world of community organizing and activist politics. Those experiences informed what he now aims to do in terms of creating innovative technology that informs, connects, and empowers people all over the world.
